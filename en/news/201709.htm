Opportunity for Master’s Degree with Scholarship Majoring in Computer Science @ University of Jinan, China   

 

Laboratory of Computational Intelligence and Image Processing, School of Information Science & Engineering, University of Jinan (UJN), China, is seeking highly motivated MS students with three years’ support of scholarship (Chinese Government Scholarship, Shandong Government Scholarship, UJN President Scholarship).

Our laboratory is looking for students who are interested in machine learning, intelligent computing, Image Processing.For more information, please refer to (Sijie Niu's Homepages).

Eligibility: Applicants must:
  （1）Be a citizen of a country other than the People’s Republic of China, and be in good health;
  （2）Not be a registered student in any Chinese universities at the time of application;
  （3）Be a BS or BEng holder (under the age of 35) in the field related to Computer Science/Engineering, Information and Communication Technologies, or Mathematics;
  （4）Be proficient in English Language (reading, writing and speaking);
  （5）Have excellent academic back ground, preferably GPA 3.5 and above.

Scholarship Coverage:
  （1）A waiver of fee for registration, tuition, internship and basic textbooks;
  （2）Free on-campus accommodation;
  （3）Medical insurance and protection scheme for international students in China;
  （4）Monthly living allowance (Amount is dependent on qualifications and experience).

Application Deadline:
Applicants should send a cover letter, detailed resume (basic info plus photo, marital status, GPA, education, professional expertise, research experience, publications if any, etc), with academic transcripts enclosed, ASAP for consideration and further processing. The application (via email) deadline is March 20th, 2018.
