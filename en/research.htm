My research team is termed as CIIP. 'CIIP' means 'Computational Intelligence Image Processing '. It is a Lab for Image Computational from School of Information Science and Engineering, University of Jinan, Jinan, China.

CIIP dedicates to pursuing fundamental research and engineering application of Computational Intelligence methods to creating innovative solutions. The special interests of CIIP include

  • CI Model and Algorithm (Evolutionary Optimization, Deep Network Learning, Level Set)

  • CI-based Network Analytics (Network Structure, Network Robustness, Network Prediction)

  • CI-based Medical Image Analysis (Segmentation, Feature Learning, Prediction)

  • Various Real-world Applications
